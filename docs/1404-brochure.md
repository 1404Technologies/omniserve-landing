## Who We Are

1404 Technologies Limited is a global Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and
software product company helping U.S. companies reduce operational costs,
scale efficiently, and maintain regulatory compliance. With headquarters in
London and subsidiaries in the U.S. and Nigeria, we deliver enterprise-grade
services across IT, cybersecurity, and customer support — plus our flagship
SaaS product, OmniServe.

## Our Product: OmniServe

OmniServe is a unified operations platform that eliminates software sprawl and
connects your business tools — from project management and CRM to accounting
and compliance — in one intelligent hub. Built for UK and U.S. SMEs, it
automates workflows, provides real-time cross-system dashboards, and ensures
audit-ready compliance.

### Key Features

- Universal integration layer (Xero, QuickBooks, Salesforce, Trello, MS Project, and 50+ more)
- No-code workflow automation
- Unified dashboards combining operational and financial data
- SaaS management module to discover and optimize subscriptions
- Compliance engine with audit trails and MTD/HIPAA/GDPR support
- Industry modules for Construction, Logistics, and Healthcare

### Client Benefits

- Save up to 5 hours per user per week
- Reduce SaaS waste by identifying unused licenses
- Achieve audit-ready compliance automatically
- Connect field teams to office systems in real time
